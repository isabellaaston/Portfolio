About Me.

I come from a career in Marketing, but decided to make the move into software development as I didn't feel fulfilled. I am really enjoying learning about lots of new technologies, as well as their implementation and look forward to a career full of continuous learning and development.


I thrive in a team, and really get my energy from collaborating with others, and have loved working on projects with my course mates at Mayden Academy.


Having grown up in Spain, I know that the key to a good life is great company and good food, which means that in my spare time, I love spending time in the kitchen, and entertaining friends.
